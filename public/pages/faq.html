Account & Profile

How do I create a GameSquad account?

Creating a GameSquad account is easy:

  1. Click on the "Register" button in the top-right corner of the homepage
  2. Enter your email address, desired username, and password
  3. Review and accept our Terms of Service and Privacy Policy
  4. Click "Create Account"
  5. Verify your email address by clicking the link in the confirmation email we send you

After these steps, you'll be able to log in and set up your gaming profile.

How do I set up my gaming profile?

To set up your gaming profile:

  1. Log into your GameSquad account
  2. Click on your username in the top-right corner and select "My Profile"
  3. Click on "Edit Profile"
  4. Add information about your gaming preferences, including:
    • Games you play
    • Your rank in each game
    • Languages you speak
    • Your playing style and preferences
  5. Upload a profile picture (optional)
  6. Click "Save Changes"

A complete profile will help you connect with compatible teammates!

How do I reset my password?

If you've forgotten your password:

  1. Go to the login page
  2. Click on "Forgot password?"
  3. Enter your email address
  4. Check your email for a password reset link
  5. Click the link and follow the instructions to create a new password

If you don't receive the reset email, check your spam folder or contact support for assistance.

Can I change my username?

Yes, you can change your username once every 30 days:

  1. Go to your profile page
  2. Click on "Edit Profile"
  3. Click on "Change Username"
  4. Enter your desired new username
  5. Click "Save Changes"

Note that your previous username will become available for other users to claim.

Lobbies & Matchmaking

How do I create a lobby?

To create a new lobby:

  1. Click on "Create Lobby" in the navigation menu
  2. Set your lobby details:
    • Lobby name
    • Game
    • Player count (max 5)
    • Rank requirements
    • Language preferences
    • Description
  3. Choose if you want the lobby to be public or private
  4. Click "Create Lobby"

Your lobby will then be visible to other players who match your criteria (if public) or only to those you invite (if private).

How do I join a lobby?

To join an existing lobby:

  1. Click on "Lobbies" in the navigation menu
  2. Browse the available lobbies or use filters to find one that matches your preferences
  3. Click on a lobby to view details
  4. Click "Join Lobby"

If the lobby is full or has specific requirements you don't meet, you won't be able to join. Private lobbies require an invitation from the host.

How does matchmaking work?

GameSquad uses several factors to match players:

  • Game preferences: The games you've selected in your profile
  • Rank: Your skill level in specific games
  • Language: The languages you speak
  • Playing style: Your preferred playstyle (casual, competitive, etc.)
  • Online status: Whether you're currently online and available

When you search for lobbies or players, our system will prioritize those that best match your profile information. The more complete your profile, the better matches you'll receive.

Friends & Messaging

How do I add someone as a friend?

To add someone as a friend:

  1. Find the player through the "Players" search or in a lobby
  2. Visit their profile
  3. Click the "Add Friend" button
  4. Optionally, add a personal message
  5. Wait for them to accept your request

Once accepted, they'll appear in your friend list and you'll see their online status.

How do I send private messages?

To send a private message to another user:

  1. Navigate to the Messages page by clicking on "Messages" in your profile dropdown
  2. Select a friend from your list or click "New Message"
  3. Type your message in the text box
  4. Press Enter or click the send button

You can only send private messages to users who are in your friends list.

Technical Issues

What browsers are supported?

GameSquad supports the following browsers:

  • Google Chrome (recommended) - version 88 or higher
  • Mozilla Firefox - version 85 or higher
  • Microsoft Edge - version 88 or higher
  • Safari - version 14 or higher

For the best experience, we recommend using the latest version of Google Chrome with WebRTC enabled for voice chat features.

Why am I experiencing connection issues?

Connection issues can be caused by several factors:

  • Unstable internet connection
  • Browser cache or cookie issues
  • Firewall or network restrictions
  • Server maintenance (check our status page)

Try these troubleshooting steps:

  1. Refresh the page
  2. Clear your browser cache and cookies
  3. Disable browser extensions
  4. Check your internet connection
  5. Try a different browser

If problems persist, please contact our support team with details about your issue.

Billing & Subscriptions

Is GameSquad free to use?

Yes, GameSquad is completely free to use with a basic account. This includes:

  • Creating and joining lobbies
  • Basic matchmaking
  • In-game chat
  • Adding friends

We do offer a Premium subscription with additional features, but it's not required to enjoy the core functionality of the platform.

What's included in Premium membership?

Premium membership includes:

  • Ad-free experience
  • Priority matchmaking
  • Advanced profile customization
  • Custom status messages
  • Exclusive profile badges
  • Higher friend limit (500 vs 100)
  • Access to advanced analytics
  • Voice chat features

Premium membership costs $4.99/month or $49.99/year (save 16%).
