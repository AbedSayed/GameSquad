REPORT A BUG

Found a bug in GameSquad? Help us improve by reporting it! Detailed bug reports help our development team identify and fix issues more quickly. Please provide as much information as possible.

Tips for a Good Bug Report

  • Be specific about what happened and when it occurred
  • Include steps to reproduce the issue
  • Mention what you expected to happen versus what actually happened
  • List your device type, browser, and operating system
  • Add screenshots or recordings if possible
