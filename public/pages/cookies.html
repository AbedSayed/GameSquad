COOKIE POLICY

1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer, smartphone, or other device when you visit our website. They allow us to recognize your device and remember certain information about your visit, such as your preferences and actions.

Cookies are widely used to make websites work more efficiently, provide a better browsing experience, and give website owners information about how their sites are used.

2. How We Use Cookies

GameSquad uses cookies for several reasons:

  • Essential cookies: Required for the basic functioning of our website, such as user authentication and security
  • Preference cookies: Remember your preferences and settings to enhance your experience
  • Performance cookies: Collect information about how you use our website, which helps us improve its functionality
  • Analytics cookies: Help us understand how visitors interact with our platform
  • Functionality cookies: Allow the website to remember choices you make and provide enhanced features

3. Types of Cookies We Use

Cookie Name Type Purpose Duration
session_id Essential Maintains your session and authentication status Session
remember_me Functionality Remembers your login information for future visits 30 days
theme_preference Preference Stores your theme preference 1 year
_ga Analytics Google Analytics - used to distinguish users 2 years

4. Managing Cookies

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. You can typically find these settings in the "Options" or "Preferences" menu of your browser. You can:

  • Delete all cookies
  • Block all cookies
  • Allow all cookies
  • Block third-party cookies
  • Clear cookies when you close your browser

Please note that restricting cookies may impact your experience on our website, as some features may not function properly.

5. Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies are placed by third-party services that appear on our pages. We use these third-party services to:

  • Analyze how our website is used so we can improve it
  • Improve our platform and detect errors
  • Allow for social media integration

These third parties may use cookies, web beacons, and similar technologies to collect information about your use of our website. This information may be used by them to provide analytical services or target advertisements.

6. Cookie Consent

When you first visit our website, you will be shown a cookie banner informing you about our use of cookies and asking for your consent where required by law.

You can change your cookie preferences at any time by clicking on the "Cookie Settings" link in the footer of our website.

7. Changes to This Cookie Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes in technology, regulation, or our business practices. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
